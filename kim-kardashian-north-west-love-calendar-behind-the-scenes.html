Kim Kardashian and North West Cozy Up on Set

Kim Kardashian West

Earlier this week, Kim Kardashian was featured in Love magazine's advent calendar in a steamy clip that showed the reality star in nothing but lingerie and fur under the Northern Lights. Today, her hairstylist César Ramirêz shared some behind-the-scenes images from the shoot, which took place in September. (Three-year-old North West makes an appearance, too.)

"The Love Advent shoot a few months ago was DOPE!" he wrote on her website and app. Ramirêz revealed that he and Kim wanted to try "something new" for new look, so he applied some clip-in extensions to her hair, which explained her super-long locks.

Kim Kardashian West

"The set was a green screen, so we had to be creative and imagine the scenery that would be edited in later," he said, describing how the shoot took place. "We were shooting in an indoor studio, so I had to work the fans to create the illusion of wind blowing her hair as if she were really walking through the Northern Lights."

Kim Kardashian West

"Kim was perfectly seductive in her movements, which helped make everything come together. I loved everything about this look for her," Ramirêz added. "I think she really shows us a simple but sexy mysterious side here." And that she did.
