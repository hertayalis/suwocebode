Gamer Rob Luna Passes Away,Rob Luna Obituary and Funeral

The Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League Philippines (MPL PH) has lost one of its most famous voices.

His death was announced on Facebook by ULVL, the production business where he had been employed since 2021. He was 26 at the time.

ULVL stated in a statement that they wanted to pay tribute to his life and to the gaming community.

In addition to serving as ULVL’s Production Manager since 2021, they said, “He was a brother to the management and a father to the employees.”

Many esports personalities have paid tribute to Rob Luna on Facebook by sharing their happy recollections of him.

Bennette “Prof B” Felix paid tribute to the person he considers to be his closest friend in the business in a touching Facebook statement.

Rob was the shoutcaster who also wanted to develop on YouTube and other platforms, so I can say that he brought the New Flavor or Meta to the MLBB scene, he wrote.

Rob was courageous enough to fight this. “He fought his illness and requested not to make it public because he didn’t want to cause any commotion.

MPL Casters like JC “Naisou” Rezabek and Caisam “Wolf” Nopueto also shared their condolences.

“The only thing I know for sure is that you are always the first to observe, encourage, and hype me up in everything I do. whatever it may be. Man, I mourn you. I appreciate everything,” remarked Wolf.

On MPL Philippines Season 4’s Tagalog broadcast, Luna made her official broadcasting debut. Additionally, he had cast the seventh season of MPL Philippines as well as foreign competitions like the Southeast Asia Cup 2021 and the M2 World Championship.
