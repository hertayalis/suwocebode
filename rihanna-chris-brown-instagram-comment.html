Chris Brown Commented on Rihanna's Instagram

Rihanna's look at the Crop Over Festival in Barbados is causing quite a stir online. Not only was the singer's look sheer perfection—a diamanté bikini, d.bleu.dazzled tights, and thigh-high gladiators—but her photos drew millions of comments and likes, as per usual. And, according to reports, Rihanna's ex, the controversial Chris Brown, was amongst her admirers.

According to E! News, Brown's comment consisted of a suggestive pair of googly eyes.
