Musical Families

Bruno's four sisters have formed a singing group called The LYLAS - which stands for Love You Like A Sister. His brother Eric plays drums in Bruno's band.
At the age of two, he joined his family's performances as an Elvis impersonator. Later on he added Michael Jackson impersonations to the act.
