No One Higher by Aaron Shust

We've just added three new original MultiTracks from Aaron Shust's new album Morning Rises which is a great collection of worshipful including his own version of "Cornerstone" originally by Hillsong Live and "No One Higher" which was made popular by North Point Music. We've also added the new radio single "God of Brilliant Lights" which is a great mid-tempo song that declares that God's love can penetrate and darkness and He never turns us away. Be sure to check out the New Song Cafe over on WorshipTogether.com and download the free lead sheet for this great new song.
