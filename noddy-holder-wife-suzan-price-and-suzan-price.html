Neville John “Noddy is an  English musician, composer, and actor Neville” Holder MBE. One of the most popular performers in the UK throughout the 1970s, he was the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist for the English rock group Slade.

Who Is Noddy Holder?

Holder, who is well-known for his distinctive and strong voice, co-wrote the majority of Slade’s songs with bassist Jim Lea, including “Mama Weer All Crazee Now,” “Cum On Feel the Noize,” and “Merry Xmas Everyone.”

He began working in radio and television after leaving Slade in 1992. He was most known for his role as the lead in the ITV comedy-drama series The Grimleys (1999–2001).

On June 15, 1946, Neville John Holder was born in the Caldmore neighbourhood, close to the heart of Walsall, Staffordshire, England.

At the age of seven, he and his family relocated to the Beechdale Estate, a council estate located north of the town, where Rob Halford also lived.

Noddy Holder Career

At the age of seven, he and his family relocated to the Beechdale Estate, a council estate located north of the town, where Rob Halford also lived.

Holder, a window cleaner’s son, completed a year at a grammar school before it closed in 1957 after passing the eleven-plus exam.

After that, he studied at the brand-new T. P. Riley Comprehensive School, where he excelled in six GCE O-level exams.

At thirteen, he got together with pals from school to establish a band named the Rockin’ Phantoms. He used the money he earned from a part-time job to purchase an amplifier and a guitar.

Additionally, he drove Robert Plant to shows with Plant’s band, the Tennessee Teens, at the time using his father’s window-cleaning van.

Holder formed his own group, the Memphis Cutouts, and in the early 1960s, he released four singles for EMI’s Columbia label with Steve Brett & the Mavericks.

Noddy Holder Wife: Suzan Price

In 1976, Holder married  Leandra Russell, a garment designer. They separated in 1984 after having two kids.

Holder wed Suzan Price, a television producer, in 2004. The two have a son named Django, who is named after Django Reinhardt. Holder currently resides in Prestbury, Cheshire.

Holder was given a six-month prognosis after receiving an oesophageal cancer diagnosis in 2018. He “responded well” to experimental chemotherapy treatment that he received at Manchester’s Christie Hospital.

In 2023, his spouse Suzan revealed the details of his cancer fight in her monthly Cheshire Life magazine column.

She said that “experts never like to use the word ‘cure’, but five years later and he’s feeling good and looking great.

Who Is Suzan Price?

Television producer Suzan Price,57, is the wife of Noddy Holder. She has produced episodes for ITV including Loose Women, formerly known as Live Talk when Price started working on the show in 2000.

IMDb notes that Suzan was also involved in the production of ITV’s All Star Mr & Mrs, a celebrity couple game show hosted by Fern Britton and Phillip Schofield that ran from 2008 to 2016.

Shake It Up, Beverly, the producer’s debut novel, was published in June 2022. She is currently a published novelist.

Lincolnshire Live claims that the Twist and Shout song by the Beatles from 1963 is the inspiration behind the book’s title.

The plot, which is set in contemporary Liverpool, centers on a 50-something Beatle fan who is searching for a partner to share the same level of love as she does for bassist Sir Paul McCartney.
