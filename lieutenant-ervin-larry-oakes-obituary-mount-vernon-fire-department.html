Lieutenant Ervin Larry Oakes Obituary: Mount Vernon Fire Department

The loss of our Lieutenant Ervin Larry Oakes has severely grieved the City of Mount Vernon and the Mount Vernon Fire Department.

Ervin “Larry” Oakes, better known by his stage name “Oak Tree,” was born and reared in Mount Vernon. Prior to joining The Mount Vernon Fire Department in 1993, he served his country as a Marine.

On Ladder 3 at Station #3, Ervin spent the most of his career as a Truck Company Fire Fighter. On March 31, 2017, “Brother Oakes” was promoted to the position of Fire Lieutenant, and on September 13, 2023, he would have reached the milestone of 30 years of service.

Obituary And Burial Arrangements

The funeral arrangements will be revealed by the family. The family and loved ones will share details about the obituary, funeral, and life celebration at the appropriate time. We will do our best to keep you updated on them.

Our heartfelt condolences go out to the deceased’s family and friends, who have been struggling with the loss of such an intelligent and compassionate individual.
