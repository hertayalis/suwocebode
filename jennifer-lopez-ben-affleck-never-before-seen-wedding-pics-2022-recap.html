Jennifer Lopez Posts Never-Before-Seen Wedding Pics with Ben Affleck

Jennifer Lopez has created an inside look at her momentous year.

The multi-hyphenate joined the 2022 recap Instagram trend yesterday, posting a sentimental peek into her past year. The video montage offers an intimate look into the special, never-before-seen moments she shared with her family this year, as well as peeks into her two summer weddings with her husband Ben Affleck.

The clip includes several loving moments between the superstar couple as it goes through the months of the year, including peeks into their wedding planning process. The first hint of prep came immediately after their April engagement, with the "Waiting For Tonight" singer including shots where she tries on a veil and views early sketches of the wedding party looks.

As the video heads into summer, we see new pics of the Afflecks' surprise Vegas wedding in July. In the photos, the bride and groom sit in a pink Cadillac convertible in front of the chapel, posing with their arms wrapped around each other. The Marry Me actress wears her second gown of the night, a lace corseted gown with long sleeves and a column skirt.

As for the couple's second Georgia wedding, the JLo Beauty founder included new pics from the lavish ceremony, including a gorgeous full shot of one of her jaw-dropping wedding gowns. In the glam photo, the style star wears a glam backless halter dress with a plunging neckline adorned with flowers. She paired the flared gown with a short veil that framed her face in a Old Hollywood-evoking style, and carried a matching white clutch.

Meanwhile, the Good Will Hunting star wears a white tuxedo jacket and black pants as he looks lovingly at his bride, standing with his arm wrapped around her.

Mrs. Affleck previously shared the details of her two ceremonies through her On The JLo newsletter, where she described the Georgia wedding weekend as "heaven."

"Years ago, we had no idea the road ahead would mean navigating so many labyrinths and hold so many surprises, blessings, and delights," she wrote. "It all culminated in this moment, one of the most perfect of our lives. We couldn't have been happier. I wish all of you the same kind of happiness...the hard-earned kind that's all the sweeter for the journey that came before it."
