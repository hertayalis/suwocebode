See Pregnant Emily Blunt's Baby Bump

Everyone needs a little retail therapy every now and then. Emily Blunt and her husband John Krasinski enjoyed some of their own when they took some time out from their busy schedules and stepped out in West Hollywood, Calif., earlier this week.

After grabbing some gear at James Perse boutique, the mom-to-be was glowing as she strolled next to the Office alum with one of the store's shopping bags in hand, camouflaging her baby bump in a flowy, floral maxi dress. Blunt completed her spring casual look with black caged sandals and black sunnies, and kept her hair simple in a bob parted down the middle.

As the duo made their way down boutique-lined Melrose Avenue, the 33-year-old Huntsman: Winter's War star palmed the arm of her husband, who opted for a plaid button-down top, jeans, black shades and black canvas shoes for their shopping date.

Much like her killer red carpet fashion, Blunt has given us major fashion inspo with her maternity street stylings as well. Just days ago, she showed off another chic look when she headed out in Los Angeles for a family day at a farmer's market. With their daughter in tow, Blunt grabbed groceries with Krasinski, sporting loose fuschia pants, a flowy white top with slits on the sides, nude gladiator sandals and a camera around her neck.

Whether her next stop is another family outing or the red carpet, we can't wait to see what she wears to it.
