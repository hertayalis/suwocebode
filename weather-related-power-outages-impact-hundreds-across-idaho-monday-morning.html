Idaho Power: Weather-related power outages impact hundreds across Idaho Monday morning

KUNA, Idaho (CBS2) — According to Idaho Power, multiple power outages are scattered throughout Idaho on Monday morning.

At 3:28 a.m., a power outage began impacting 139 people in Kuna. The cause is likely weather-related. The power has been restored.

Another outage impacting Notus and Parma started at 4:49 a.m. 425 people are currently affected. The cause is likely weather-related. A crew is on-site, and estimated restoration is expected at 8 a.m.

A third outage impacting 226 people in Emmett began at 5:24 a.m. The cause is unknown, and a crew is on-site. The power has been restored.

An outage near Hidden Springs is impacting 110 customers. The outage started at 7:10 a.m., and the cause is likely weather-related. A crew is en route to the incident. Estimated restoration is expected at 10 a.m.

There is an outage impacting 67 people in Council, which started at 7:45 a.m. The cause is unknown, and a crew is assigned to the event. Estimated restoration is expected at 10:30 a.m.

An outage impacting 16 people in Kuna/Meridian began at 7:46 a.m. The cause is under investigation, and a crew is assigned to the event. Estimated restoration is expected at 9:30 a.m.

An outage impacting 1,663 people in Eagle/Star began at 7:53 a.m. The cause is under investigation, and a crew is dispatched to the event. Estimated restoration is expected at 9 a.m.
