Born in Kielce, Swietokrzyskie

Marika Dominczyk was born in Kielce, Swietokrzyskie, Poland, on July 7, 1980 to Alejandro and Miroslaw ‘Mirek’ Dominczyk . When she was a child, she moved to the United States with her family.

Dominczyk’s father was the leader of the Polish solidarity movement. She has two sisters Dagmara Dominczyk and Veronika Dominczyk . They are both actress.
