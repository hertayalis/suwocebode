Nicole Wallace children-Spanish actress and TV presenter, Nicole Alejandra Wallace del Barrio was born on March 22, 2002, in Madrid, Spain.

Does Nicole Wallace have any children?

As of the time of filing this report, Nicole Wallace has no children. There haven’t been any reports of her adopting any child as well.

Nicole Wallace career

Wallace began her career in short films, making her small screen debut opposite Grey’s Anatomy star Tessa Ferrer in the 2008 horror short Excision.

She kept making short films and starred with horror movie icon Robert Englund, best known for playing Freddy Krueger in the Nightmare on Elm Street series in Campfire Creatures: The Skull of Sam as Vicky.

Wallace became well-known in 2018 when she was chosen to play Nora Grace in the Movistar+ series Skam España, which was adapted from the Norwegian teen series Skam.

Her character, Nora, and Celia Monedero’s character, Viri, were the focal points of the third season of the show. FesTVal de Vitoria granted the “Cima TV Award for Equality” to Nora and Viri’s season in particular for their promotion of feminism and empowerment to a young audience.

Wallace’s breakthrough performance as Nora led to features in other magazines, including SModa, Glamour, and Vogue. She has carried over her popularity into other playing parts, appearing in the drama Parot on TVE in 2021 and the romantic comedy My Fault on Prime Video in 2023.

In both movies, she plays Noah, who gets sucked into a prohibited relationship with her stepbrother Nick, who is involved in the risky street racing scene.

Gabriel Guevara, who costarred with Wallace in Skam España, plays Nick. My Fault is based on Mercedes Ron’s well-known novel series Culpables, which she first published on Wattpad.

My Fault was released in June 2023, and according to Amazon Prime Video, it had the best three-day audience stats of any non-English local original film in the service’s history.

Then, in July 2023, Prime Video announced that it would make two further films, Your Fault and Our Fault, which are based on the last two books of Mercedes Ron’s trilogy. Wallace was confirmed to play Noah again in these two productions.

On August 31, 2023, Your Fault and Our Fault started producing simultaneously. Wallace debuted in the Steffy Argelich film Vera the same month. In the movie, Wallace plays Vera, a teenage girl who is coming to terms with her sexuality when a young man stays with her and her mother on an island retreat, apart from his responsibilities as a son and a lawyer.

She has also continued to appear in a number of additional short films, such as Rafa Pavón’s 2019 Ray Sparks and Javier Alonso’s 2021 La Fuerza del Destino.

In addition, Wallace will play Alba in the upcoming television miniseries Ni una más, which is based on the same-titled novel by Miguel Sáez Carral. Ni una más is scheduled for Netflix distribution.
