Rapper AKA Family: Wife, Children, Parents, Siblings

Kiernan Jarryd Forbes, who was popularly known as AKA, was born on 28 January 1988 in Cape Town, Cape Province in South Africa. AKA was a professional singer who is known for hip hop rapping. AKA rose to fame following the release of his single Victory Lap in 2010.

AKA was reported dead after he was shot dead on Florida Road in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal in South Africa. AKA was 35 years old at the time of his death

Rapper AKA Family: Wife

Kiernan Forbes was never married but had relationships with women including Bonang Matheba, DJ Zinhle, Anele Tembe who died in 2021, and Nadia Nakai. AKA was in a relationship with Nakai until his death.

Rapper AKA Family: Children

Rapper AKA had a daughter in 2015 with DJ Zinhle whose name is Kairo Olwethu Forbes.

Rapper AKA Family: Parents

Rapper AKA was born to Lynn Forbes, his mother, and Tony Forbes, who is his father.

Rapper AKA Family: Siblings

It is currently not known if AKA had any siblings at the time of his death.
