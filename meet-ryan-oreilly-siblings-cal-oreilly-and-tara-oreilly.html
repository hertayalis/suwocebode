Ryan O’Reilly Biography

Ryan O’Reilly was born in Canada on February 7, 1991.

 People born on February 7 fall under the zodiac sign of Aquarius. His zodiac animal is Goat.

Canadian center who was selected 33rd overall in the 2009 NHL Draft by the Colorado Avalanche.

He played for the Buffalo Sabres from 2015 to 2018 before being traded to the St. Louis Blues. He helped the Blues with the 2019 Stanley Cup Championship and received the Conn Smythe as the MVP of the 2019 playoffs.

He became teammates with Vladimir Tarasenko on the Blues. Before he made his NHL debut, he spent two years in the OHL playing for the Erie Otters.

Ryan played previously for other teams in the NHL. The teams include, Metallurg Magnitogorsk and Buffalo Sabres.

He played a vital role in his team to win several championships and tournaments throughout his career as a Hockey player. He is recognized as one of the NHL’s best face-off men and two-way centers.

Ryan tied the knot as a husband with Dayna Douros on June 30, 2018.

Ryan O’Reilly‘s fortune is estimated to be $15.4 million. The massive wealth that he has amassed comprises his income from salary as an NHL player, endorsements, and several other ventures.

Ryan and his was have a child, called Jameson Keane O’Reilly. He was born on December 23, 2017.
