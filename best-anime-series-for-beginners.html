7 Anime Series That Are Perfect For Beginners To Watch

Japanese animated series, or anime, have steadily gained popularity over the years and now encompass a wide and varied library of content that has something for everyone.

These animated shows, often based on mangas or web comics, feature complex plots, incredible world-building, and stunning art.

Most of us have limited experience with anime, mostly restricted to Studio Ghibli movies and after-school Pokemon reruns on Cartoon Network. More recently, animes like Demon Slayer and Netflix’s recently released live-action One Piece series have popularised the medium once again.

But if you’re a new entrant who is looking to explore these works, the sheer amount of options might seem daunting. On top of that, the cultural differences and unique quirks of these series can intimidate a non-anime fan. However, there are many series that can be your gateway to the world of anime without requiring a lot of insider knowledge about the medium.
