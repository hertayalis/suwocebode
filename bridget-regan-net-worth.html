Bridget Regan Net Worth

Bridget Regan net worth:

$2 million

Bridget Regan is an American actress whose Hollywood career began in the mid 2000s. After appearing in two episodes of Law & Order: Criminal Intent, she appeared in the movie The Babysitters. She had a small role in Sex and the City and appeared as a recurring character on The Black Donnellys. Her first starring role on TV was on Legend of the Seeker. In the 2010s, she had recurring roles on Beauty & the Beast, White Collar, Jane the Virgin, and Agent Carter. She now stars on The Last Ship starting Season 3. Additionally, she starred in the HBO film The Leisure Class.
